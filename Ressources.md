## **Curated List of Resources**

- **On Operating Systems & Networking:**
    
    - [_Modern Operating Systems_ by **Andrew S. Tanenbaum**](https://csc-knu.github.io/sys-prog/books/Andrew%20S.%20Tanenbaum%20-%20Modern%20Operating%20Systems.pdf)

    - [Computer Networks by Andrew S. Tanenbaum](https://csc-knu.github.io/sys-prog/books/Andrew%20S.%20Tanenbaum%20-%20Computer%20Networks.pdf)

	- [Distributed Systems by Andrew S. Tanenbaum](https://alex-nguyen.github.io/vinhtngu/CSCourses/Spring2020/CS5352/Distributed_Systems_3-200225.pdf)

    - _Operating Systems: Three Easy Pieces_ by **Remzi Arpaci-Dusseau & Andrea Arpaci-Dusseau** see [[operating-systems-three-easy-pieces-paperbacknbsped-198508659x-9781985086593_compress.pdf]] [download online](https://dokumen.pub/qdownload/operating-systems-three-easy-pieces-paperbacknbsped-198508659x-9781985086593.html)

    - [TCP/IP Illustrated_ series by **W. Richard Stevens](https://www.r-5.org/files/books/computers/internals/net/Richard_Stevens-TCP-IP_Illustrated-EN.pdf)**

    - Research papers and articles by **Van Jacobson** (especially on congestion control)

- **On Data Structures & Algorithms:**
    
    - [_The Art of Computer Programming_ by **Donald E. Knuth**](http://broiler.astrometry.net/~kilian/The_Art_of_Computer_Programming%20-%20Vol%201.pdf)

    - [_Algorithms_ by **Robert Sedgewick**](https://dn790005.ca.archive.org/0/items/algorithhms-4th-edition-by-robert-sedgewick-kevin-wayne/Algorithhms%204th%20Edition%20by%20Robert%20Sedgewick%2C%20Kevin%20Wayne.pdf)
    
- **On Systems and Concurrency:**
    
    - _The Art of Multiprocessor Programming_ by **Maurice Herlihy and Nir Shavit**

    - Technical blog posts and discussions by **Mooly Sagiv** and others who focus on formal verification and concurrency challenges

- **On Programming Languages & Building from Scratch:**
    
    - _The Rust Programming Language_ (“The Book”) – especially chapters on low-level systems programming by **Aaron Turon** and **Nicholas Matsakis**.

    - _Programming Elixir_ by **Dave Thomas** (focus on low-level implementations)

    - Writings and talks by **Joe Armstrong** (for insights into the actor model without abstracting away the foundational ideas)

- **On Software Architecture & DevOps:**
    
    - _Design Patterns: Elements of Reusable Object-Oriented Software_ by **Gamma et al.**

    - _Site Reliability Engineering_ by the Google SRE team (technical chapters only)

    - Technical posts on observability and distributed systems by **Charity Majors** and **Liz Fong-Jones**
